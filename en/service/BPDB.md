# BPDB

> Beep—beep—
> Welcome back to the millennium!

::: warning
This service may be in testing. It can be unstable and may introduce breaking changes at any time.
:::

BPDB is a **DN42 third-party database** for managing AS-SET objects, aiming to simplify the registration and maintenance of AS-SETs.

*Please note: This project has no direct connection to the dn42-registry, and any submissions will not be automatically reflected in the DN42 registry.*

See also: [BPDB in DN42 Git](https://git.dn42.dev/BPDB/BPDB)
